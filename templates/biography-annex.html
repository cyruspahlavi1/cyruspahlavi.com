BIOGRAPHY
His Royal Highness Cyrus Pahlavi
Identity and Lineage
His Royal Highness Cyrus Pahlavi is a member of the Pahlavi family. Public sources describe him as the son of Prince Shahram Pahlavi-Nia and Niloufar Afshar, and the grandson of Her Imperial Highness Princess Ashraf Pahlavi (twin sister of Mohammad Reza Shah Pahlavi).
Public Work and Focus
HRH Cyrus Pahlavi's public advocacy focuses on secular governance, equal citizenship, women's full equality, national unity, and a peaceful foreign policy anchored in non-aggression and law. The Office emphasizes documentation of abuses, protection of civilians, and lawful accountability—rejecting collective punishment and revenge.
Education and Professional Background
Some publicly available biographies associated with cultural and arts venues state that HRH Cyrus Pahlavi studied in Switzerland and later pursued art and design studies. The Office will publish a verified education and professional record as supporting documentation is consolidated for governmental and institutional distribution.
His documented professional work includes:
  • Visual artist with exhibitions featured on international platforms including Artnet
  • Actor with verified credits including "Ellie Parker" (2005) featuring Naomi Watts
  • Cultural advocate and founding chair of MIGA DAO, focused on preserving Persian heritage
  • Collaborator with luxury brands on artistic and cultural projects
Diplomatic and Policy Engagement
His Royal Highness has engaged with diplomatic representatives, government officials, and international organizations on matters concerning Iran and the broader Middle East region. His approach emphasizes:
  • Advocacy for secular democratic governance in Iran
  • Support for women's rights and gender equality
  • Promotion of rule of law and human rights
  • Peaceful transition and national reconciliation
  • Non-aggression and regional stability
Languages and Cultural Heritage
HRH Cyrus Pahlavi positions Iran's constitutional heritage and civilizational identity as central to a democratic future: a state restrained by law, pluralism protected by citizenship, and dignity secured for every Iranian.
Current Role and Mission
The Office of His Royal Highness Cyrus Pahlavi advances a peaceful, lawful, and nationally legitimate transition for Iran—toward a secular constitutional order that protects equal citizenship, human dignity, women's full equality, national unity, and peaceful relations with neighbors.
Operating principles include legality, non-violence, equal citizenship, national unity, protection of civilians, accountability, and regional peace through non-aggression and economic cooperation.
This biography contains only information that can be publicly verified or defended. Claims regarding educational credentials and diplomatic roles reflect publicly available sources and should be independently confirmed for official purposes.
