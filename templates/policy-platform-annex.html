POLICY PLATFORM
His Royal Highness Cyrus Pahlavi
I. SECULAR DEMOCRATIC GOVERNANCE
Establishment of a constitutional democracy that separates religious and state institutions while protecting freedom of belief for all Iranians.
  • Constitutional framework guaranteeing democratic elections, separation of powers, and judicial independence
  • Protection of religious, ethnic, and linguistic minorities through constitutional safeguards
  • Decentralization of power to empower local governance and regional autonomy
  • Transparent institutions with strong anti-corruption mechanisms
II. RULE OF LAW AND HUMAN RIGHTS
Commitment to universal human rights standards and the establishment of independent judicial institutions.
  • Abolition of arbitrary detention, torture, and capital punishment for political offenses
  • Freedom of expression, assembly, and association for all citizens
  • Independent judiciary free from political interference
  • Truth and reconciliation process to address past injustices
  • International cooperation on human rights monitoring and protection
III. WOMEN'S EQUALITY AND EMPOWERMENT
Full legal, social, and economic equality for women and girls in all aspects of Iranian society.
  • Constitutional guarantee of gender equality and non-discrimination
  • Equal access to education, employment, and political participation
  • Legal protection against gender-based violence and discrimination
  • Women's freedom of dress, movement, and personal choice
  • Support for women's economic empowerment and entrepreneurship
IV. ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT AND SOCIAL JUSTICE
Market-based economic development combined with strong social safety nets to ensure prosperity and opportunity for all Iranians.
  • Free market economy with robust anti-monopoly protections
  • Investment in education, healthcare, and social infrastructure
  • Environmental protection and sustainable development practices
  • Support for small and medium enterprises and innovation
  • Social safety net for vulnerable populations
V. PEACEFUL INTERNATIONAL RELATIONS
Non-aggressive foreign policy focused on regional stability, international cooperation, and peaceful resolution of disputes.
  • Commitment to international law and multilateral cooperation
  • Peaceful relations with all neighbors and regional partners
  • Support for nuclear non-proliferation and weapons of mass destruction disarmament
  • Active participation in international organizations and treaties
  • People-to-people diplomacy and cultural exchange
VI. NATIONAL UNITY AND RECONCILIATION
Healing the divisions created by decades of authoritarian rule through inclusive governance and national dialogue.
  • National reconciliation process involving all segments of Iranian society
  • Protection and celebration of Iran's cultural and linguistic diversity
  • Integration of diaspora Iranians in national reconstruction efforts
  • Transitional justice mechanisms that prioritize healing and accountability
  • Educational reforms promoting critical thinking and civic engagement
