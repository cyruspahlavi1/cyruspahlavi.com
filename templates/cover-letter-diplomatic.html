Your Excellency / Dear [Title] [Surname],
I write to you on behalf of His Royal Highness Cyrus Pahlavi to bring to your attention the urgent situation facing the Iranian people and to request your government's continued support for human rights and democratic principles in Iran.
As detailed in the accompanying declaration, the Iranian regime continues its systematic suppression of peaceful protesters, arbitrary detention of dissidents, and denial of fundamental freedoms to the Iranian people. The international community's unified response is essential to address these grave violations of human rights and international law.
Specific Requests:
1. Humanitarian Action: We respectfully request your government's support for independent investigations into documented cases of arbitrary detention, torture, and extrajudicial killings. The Iranian people deserve justice and accountability for these crimes against humanity.
2. Targeted Measures: We urge the implementation of targeted sanctions against regime officials responsible for human rights violations, while explicitly avoiding measures that would harm the Iranian people's access to food, medicine, and communications.
3. Digital Freedom: We call for robust support of internet freedom initiatives and protection for Iranian journalists, lawyers, doctors, and human rights defenders who face persecution for their peaceful activities.
4. Diplomatic Pressure: We encourage your government to use all available diplomatic channels to demand the immediate release of prisoners of conscience and an end to the regime's violent crackdown on peaceful expression.
The Iranian people's courageous struggle for freedom, dignity, and democratic governance deserves the sustained support of the international community. Your leadership in defending human rights and democratic values can make a meaningful difference in this critical moment.
I would welcome the opportunity to discuss these matters further at your convenience. The Office of His Royal Highness Cyrus Pahlavi stands ready to engage constructively with your government on policies that support the Iranian people's aspirations for freedom and human dignity.
Thank you for your consideration of this urgent matter and your continued commitment to universal human rights.
Respectfully,
His Royal Highness Cyrus Pahlavi
